孔金鸥电磁波理论笔记

序言

  • 立足麦克斯韦方程组及其本构关系形式不变的立场，阐述了在狭义相对论原理指导下的同意的宏观电磁理论
  • 强调电磁波矢量$\overline{k}​$
  • 引入一个基本单位空间频率$\mathbf{K}{\mathrm{o}}=2 \pi \mathrm{m}^{-1}$，代表每米空间中电磁波变换的周期数，则$k=1\mathbf{K}{0}$时间频率为300 $\mathrm{MH} \mathrm{z}$
  • 符号上矢量$\overline{k}$，单位矢量$\hat{A}$，$\overline{A}=\hat{x} A_{x}+\hat{y} A_{y}+\hat{z} A_{z}​$

第一章 基本概念

  • 1.1自由空间中的麦克斯韦方程组（安培定律，法拉第定律。电场与磁场的高斯定律）

$$
\begin{aligned} \nabla \times \overline{E}(\overline{r}, t)+\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \overline{B}(\overline{r}, t) &=0 \ \nabla \times \overline{H}(\vec{r}, t)-\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \overline{D}(\overline{r}, t) &=\overline{J}(\overline{r}, t) \ \nabla \cdot \overline{B}(\overline{r}, t) &=0 \ \nabla \cdot \overline{D}(\overline{r}, t) &=\rho(\overline{r}, t) \end{aligned}
$$

电流连续性方程:$\mathbf{\nabla} \cdot J(\boldsymbol{r}, t)=-\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \rho (\boldsymbol{r}, t)$

自由空间本构关系

$D=\varepsilon_{\mathrm{a}} \boldsymbol{E}$
$\boldsymbol{B}=\mu_{\mathrm{o}} \boldsymbol{H}$

$\varepsilon_{0} \approx 8.85 \times 10^{-12}(\mathbf{F} / \mathbf{m}), \mu_{0} \approx 4 \pi \times 10^{-7}(\mathbf{H} / \mathbf{m})​$

自由空间无源区域下
$$
\begin{array}{c}{\nabla \times H=\epsilon_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} E} \ {\nabla \times E=-\mu_0 \frac{\partial}{\partial t} H}\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l}{\nabla \cdot E=0} \ {\nabla \cdot H=0}\end{array}
$$

即 $$
\begin{array}{l}{\frac{\partial}{\partial y} H_{z}-\frac{\partial}{\partial z} H_{y}=\varepsilon_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} E_{x}} \ {\frac{\partial}{\partial z} H_{x}-\frac{\partial}{\partial x} H_{z}=\varepsilon_{u} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} E_{y}} \ {\frac{\partial}{\partial x} H_{y}-\frac{\partial}{\partial y} H_{x}=\epsilon_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} E_{z}}\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l}{\frac{\partial}{\partial y} E_{z}-\frac{\partial}{\partial z} E_{y}=-\mu_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} H_{x}} \ {\frac{\partial}{\partial z} E_{x}-\frac{\partial}{\partial x} E_{z}=-\mu_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} H_{y}} \ {\frac{\partial}{\partial x} E_{y}-\frac{\partial}{\partial y} E_{x}=-\mu_{0} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} H_z}\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{c}{\frac{\partial}{\partial x} E_{x}+\frac{\partial}{\partial y} E_{y}+\frac{\partial}{\partial z} E_{z}=0} \ {\frac{\partial}{\partial x} H_{x}+\frac{\partial}{\partial y} H_{y}+\frac{\partial}{\partial z} H_{z}=0}\end{array}
$$

Helmholtz波动方程
$$
\nabla^{2} \boldsymbol{E}-\mu_{\mathrm{0}} \varepsilon_{\mathrm{0}} \frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial t^{2}} \boldsymbol{E}=\mathbf{0}
$$
波动方程的解：$when E_{y}=E_{z}=0​$,

$\boldsymbol{E}=\hat{\boldsymbol{x}} E_{x}(z, t)​$,$\frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial z^{2}} E_{x}-\mu_{0} \varepsilon_{0} \frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial t^{2}} E_{x}=0​$
$$
E=\hat{x} E_{x}(z, t)=\hat{x} E_{0} \cos (k z-\omega t)
$$

$$
k^{2}=\omega^{2} \mu_{0} \varepsilon_{0}
$$

$ \omega T=2 \pi$, $f=1 / T$,$f=\frac{\omega}{2 \pi}$,单位Hz,$\mathbf{1} \mathbf{H}z=\mathbf{1} \mathbf{s}^{-\mathbf{1}}$,$ \omega $ 为波角频率，$\omega{0}=2 \pi s^{-1}$，同理对空间频率K，$k \lambda=2 \pi$，$k=\frac{2 \pi}{\lambda}$，$\mathbf{K}{\mathrm{o}}=2 \pi \mathrm{m}^{-1}$，$k=1\mathbf{K}{0}$则时间频率为300 $\mathrm{MH} \mathrm{z}$，$\omega=ck$,主要研究$0.01\mathbf{K}{0}-100\mathbf{K}{0}$,

$k=A \mathrm{K}{\mathrm{o}}$,$\lambda=2 \pi / k=2 \pi / A K{0}=1 / A (m)$,

$f=c k / 2 \pi=c A K_{0} / 2 \pi=3 \times 10^{8} A \mathrm{Hz}$,

光子能量$\hbar \omega=\hbar c k$，$\hbar=1.05 \times 10^{-34} \mathrm{J} / \mathrm{s}=h/2\pi$，$q=1.6 \times 10^{-19} \mathrm{C}$,$\hbar \omega=\hbar c A \mathrm{K}_{0}=(\hbar c A \mathrm{K} / q) \mathrm{eV} \approx 1.24\times 10^-6 A \ eV$

矢量分析与边界条件
$$
\begin{array}{c}{C \cdot(\boldsymbol{A} \times \boldsymbol{B})=\boldsymbol{A} \cdot(\boldsymbol{B} \times \boldsymbol{C})=\boldsymbol{B} \cdot(\boldsymbol{C} \times \boldsymbol{A})} \ {\boldsymbol{C} \times(\boldsymbol{A} \times \boldsymbol{B})=\boldsymbol{A}(\boldsymbol{C} \cdot \boldsymbol{B})-(\boldsymbol{C} \cdot \boldsymbol{A}) \boldsymbol{B}}\end{array}
$$

$$
\mathbf{V}=\hat{\boldsymbol{x}} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}+\hat{\boldsymbol{y}} \frac{\partial}{\partial y}+\hat{z} \frac{\partial}{\partial z}
$$

微分算符

标量的梯度：
$$
\nabla\Phi=\hat{\boldsymbol{x}} \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \Phi+\hat{\boldsymbol{y}} \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \Phi+\hat{\boldsymbol{z}} \frac{\partial}{\partial_{z}} \Phi\ \phi 为标量
$$

$$
\boldsymbol{E}=-\nabla \Phi,\Phi 电位函数
$$

矢量的散度
$$
\begin{aligned} \mathbf{\nabla} \cdot \boldsymbol{D} &=\left(\hat{\boldsymbol{x}} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}+\hat{\boldsymbol{y}} \frac{\partial}{\partial y}+\hat{\boldsymbol{z}} \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\right) \cdot\left(\hat{\boldsymbol{x}} D_{x}+\hat{\boldsymbol{y}} D_{y}+\hat{\boldsymbol{z}} D_{z}\right) \ &=\frac{\partial}{\partial x} D_{x}+\frac{\partial}{\partial y} D_{y}+\frac{\partial}{\partial z} D_{z} \end{aligned}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol{\nabla} \cdot \boldsymbol{D}=\lim _{\Delta \mathbf{v} \rightarrow 0} \frac{\oiint \mathbf{d} \boldsymbol{S} \cdot \boldsymbol{D}}{\Delta \boldsymbol{V}}
$$

边界条件
$$
\hat{\boldsymbol{s}} \cdot\left[\boldsymbol{D}{1}-\boldsymbol{D}{2}\right]=\rho_{\mathbf{s}}，\rho_s 为表面电荷密度
$$

$$
\hat{\boldsymbol{s}} \cdot\left[\boldsymbol{B}{1}-\boldsymbol{B}{2}\right]=0
$$

散度定理

$$
\iiint_{V} \mathrm{d} V \nabla \cdot D=\oiint_{S} \mathrm{d} S \cdot D
$$
电场高斯定理

矢量 旋度
$$
\nabla \times H=\left(\hat{x} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}+\hat{y} \frac{\partial}{\partial y}+\hat{z} \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\right) \times H
$$
